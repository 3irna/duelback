Comprehensive Rules and Policies of the Backgammon Betting Platform Introduction.
Welcome to the Duelback Betting Platform. We are committed to providing a secure, fair, and transparent environment for all users. These rules and policies are designed to ensure an enjoyable and worry-free experience for users. By registering and using this platform, users agree to adhere to all provisions outlined in this document.

1. Age Requirement and Registration
- Age Requirement: Users must be at least 18 years old to participate in the games. Participation of individuals below the legal age is prohibited and their accounts will be immediately suspended.

- Identity Verification: To prevent abuse and ensure security, all users must complete the identity verification process.

- Registration information must be complete, accurate, and aligned with the individual's true identity.

2. Game Start and Turn Timing
- Dice Roll and Player Start Determination: The game begins with a dice roll, and the player with the higher number starts. This process is conducted automatically by the platform.

- Turn Time Limit: Each player has a maximum of 30 seconds to make their move. If the player fails to move within this time, the move point is transferred to the opponent.

3. Entry Fees and Reward Distribution
- Game Entry Fee: Each player must pay a specified entry fee, which is added to the game prize pool. The entry fee varies based on the type of game and competition level and is announced on the game page.

- Payments are made with Tron tokens on the TRC20 blockchain.

- Reward Distribution: Rewards are automatically and transparently transferred to the winner's account within 24 hours after the game ends.

- 90% of the prize goes to the winner, 8% to the platform, and 1% to referrals.

4. Payment Guarantee and Financial Security
- Payment Guarantee: The platform is committed to promptly and transparently paying prizes to winners. Payments are usually credited to user accounts within 24 hours.

- Financial Storage and Security: All transactions are conducted through secure gateways, and users' financial information is kept strictly confidential.

5. Disconnection Rules and Emergency Exit
- Fair Game Maintenance: In the event of an unexpected internet disconnection or emergency exit, the opposing player is considered the winner, receiving 70% of the prize, 28% goes to the platform, and 1% to referrals.

- Game Cancellation Policies: If a game is canceled due to platform technical issues, all entry fees will be refunded to the users' accounts.

6. Sportsmanship and Respect Rules
- Mutual Respect: Users must observe etiquette and respect in their communications. Insults, threats, or any inappropriate behavior will result in account suspension.

- Warnings and Penalties: Users will receive a warning for any disrespectful behavior, and repeated offenses may result in account suspension.

7. Monitoring and Fraud Prevention
- Fraud Control and Detection: Using AI and advanced algorithms, any suspicious behavior or attempt at fraud is detected.

- Blocking and Penalizing Offenders: Offending users will be permanently or temporarily banned from accessing the platform. The platform reserves the right to block a user's account balance pending investigation in case of fraud detection.

8. Penalty and Account Suspension Policy
- Penalty for Offending Users: Any illegal activity, such as fraud or use of multiple accounts, will result in a penalty and permanent account suspension.

- Grievance Process: Upon detecting a violation, the user's account will be suspended and their account balance reviewed.

Refunds will only be issued upon confirmation of no fraud.
9. Special Rewards and Incentives


- Friend Invitation Rewards: Players can earn special points by inviting friends to play.

- Daily Games and Loyalty Rewards: Users who play daily earn loyalty rewards or additional points.

- Monthly Ranking and Prizes: Special competitions and cash prizes are offered for top players each month.

10. Customer Support and Services
- 24/7 Online Support: Users can access online support 24/7 in case of issues.

- Guidance and Technical Issue Resolution: The support team promptly and effectively resolves users' technical issues.

11. Dispute Resolution and Arbitration
- Dispute Resolution Process: In the event of a dispute between players, the player can submit a ticket for support to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

- Arbitration Decision: The arbitration team's decision is final and binding, and all users must comply.

Final Terms and Conditions
These rules and policies are designed to ensure the security, fairness, and enjoyment of the gaming experience. The platform reserves the right to change or update rules with prior notice. By continuing to use the services, users automatically accept the new rules.
