  • About us

Welcome to Duel Back, where exciting competitions await you! At Duel Back, we offer a unique platform for head-to-head games, providing a different experience in the world of online gaming. Here, users can directly compete against each other and enhance their skills to reach higher levels.

Currently, Duel Back offers two popular and challenging games: backgammon and Sudoku, perfect for fans of strategic and brain-teasing games. Our aim is to introduce a new and exciting game to our lineup every month, ensuring a world of diversity and choices for our users.

What makes Duel Back special is that, in addition to enjoying the games, users can win bet prizes through two-player challenges. This feature adds an extra layer of excitement and motivation to every game.

We are committed to transparency and security in all our interactions and strive to create a safe and trustworthy environment for all players. Our support team is always ready to answer your questions and meet your needs.

Learn more about Duel Back and join us on this exciting journey. Are you ready to challenge your intellect and win great prizes? Come and meet new friends and rivals!
